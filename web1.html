How To Get Started With Card Magic

So, you want to learn how to blow peoples minds? On this website I will teach you sleight of hand techniques that are vital to being a good magician. I will teach you 4 sleight of hand techniques and then show you a trick that you can do with those techniques.

The Basics

To get started with card magic first learn the basic sleight of hand.

The Pinky Break

This is a very basic move that most beginner magicians should have under their belt its simple yet effective.

The False Cut

A cut is a move that usually mixes up the pack but as the title suggests this one does not. The move may look difficult however it is actually fairly simple and easy to do.

The Riffle Shuffle

This is one of those moves that is perfect for magicians as it is impresive and really cements the fact you are a seasoned magician. It may look difficult but with practice it will come very easily.

The Spread

This move is very important for a magician as it perfect to show the deck to a spectator and have them select a card.

Card To Top

This is where all your hard work pays off; the card trick. This trick uses all the techniques we have used up until now and brings it all together. I have performed this trick many times and its gets amazing reactions everytime.

Before you go and perform all of the new things you've learnt make sure to practice everything thoroughly and make sure it is as clean as possible before you show others; and don't forget that you are not restricted to just this trick. You can use what you've learnt to help you think of other tricks you can do.
